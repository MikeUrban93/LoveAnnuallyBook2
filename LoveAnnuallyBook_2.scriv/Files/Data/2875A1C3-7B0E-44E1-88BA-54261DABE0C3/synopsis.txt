Liz arrives at Evan's house, her heart heavy with the disappointment of her failed IVF treatment. Determined to put her own pain aside, she finds Evan slumped in his recliner, clearly under the weather.  Worried, she reminds him of his agreement to see a specialist if his health declines. Old arguments resurface, but Liz changes tactics, offering to make him his favorite childhood comfort food - fried bologna sandwiches. The familiar ritual brings a flicker of joy to both of them.
