High above Lake Michigan, Sarah finds herself captivated by the turbulent waves, a stark reflection of her own inner turmoil. Clutching an inexpensive pendant—a gift from Evan that contrasts sharply with her usual choice of luxurious jewelry—she wrestles with deep, unresolved questions. The pendant, symbolizing much more than its outward simplicity, prompts Sarah to contemplate why she treasures it so dearly, revealing her emotional struggle and the poignant significance it holds in her life.
